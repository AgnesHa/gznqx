The product consists of mask body, nose clip and mask belt
The mask body is divided into five layers:The first layer is polypropylene (PP) non-woven cloth (50g).The second layer is polypropylene (PP) ≥95 melt-sprayed cloth (25g).The third layer is polypropylene (PP) ≥95 melt-sprayed cloth (25g);The fourth layer is hot-air cotton (50g).The fifth layer is polypropylene (PP) non-woven cloth (25~30g).Nose clip with fine wire wrapped in polyethylene (PE).The mask belt is made of elastic material and the elastic material is made of nylon.
